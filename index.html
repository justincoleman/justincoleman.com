A Bit About Me

I'm a front-end developer who cares way too much about the details. The pixel that's off by one? I see it. The hover state that feels sluggish? It bothers me.

I've spent 15+ years in the space between design and code — the handoff zone where good ideas either survive or get flattened. My job is to make sure they survive. I build interfaces that feel considered, not just functional.

I've done this across e-commerce, cybersecurity, big data, nonprofits, and agencies. Different problems, same standard.
